B2B

Russian Post is your reliable partner in all areas of business shipments to Russia. Thanks to our customized logistics and fulfillment solutions, your shipments will be delivered to your destination efficiently, quickly and at a reasonable price.

Request a quote

The Vision

Optimize your B2B (business to Business) shipments to Russia with our support. We offer transparent, sustainable and scalable solutions for shipments to your customers and partners.

B2B (business to business) shipments are becoming increasingly important. Driven by advancing digitization and international networking, volumes in the B2B segment are rising continuously both within national markets and in the cross-border area. As one of the largest postal groups, Russian Post holds a broad and extensive logistics network between Europe and Russia. This network is equally available to you today for the transport of business shipments, so that you can bring your B2B shipments to Russia quickly and efficiently and scale your volumes sustainably.

The product

Similar to classic B2C (business to consumer) mail delivery, we offer you an optimal complementary delivery solution to your B2B customers. It offers you the possibility to ship goods beyond the common dimensions (maximum 30kg) as well as new product categories. With our shipping solution we help you with customs issues and enable you to achieve scalability of your volumes thanks to transparent structures. We consolidate your shipments in Berlin and bring them from there directly to Moscow, from where the distribution takes place. Depending on the product category, customer needs and delivery location, the entire supply chain is multimodal and individually coordinated.

Standard Services

  • Collection from all EU countries (in continental Europe)
  • Delivery to all locations and regions in the Russian Federation
  • Daily deliveries from Berlin to Moscow
  • Delivery times from Berlin to
    Moscow/St. Petersburg: approx. 5-8 days (average)

Additional services

  • Preparation of export declaration (customs clearance)
  • Door2Door solution
  • Storage options incl. customs warehouse
  • Fulfillment solution
  • Insurance option

Why Russian Post

  • Importance of long-term partnerships
  • Sustainable solutions
  • Transparency in the delivery process
  • Individual approach
  • Flexible discount system
  • Tailored solutions
