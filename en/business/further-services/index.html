Further services

We offer a number of other services for successful shipping to Russia. We are at your side for this and develop tailor-made solutions for you.

Logistics

We traditionally come from the postal sector and see our strengths here too. In recent years, however, we have developed into an internationally operating multimodal logistics group. Today we can offer you and your company a variety of logistics options around the globe.

Fulfillment

We also offer fulfillment solutions for our numerous e-commerce customers - both in Russia and in Europe. Our European fulfillment center currently covers an area of ​​around 1,000m², which enables us to store goods, consolidate them and then send them to Russia and Europe. The direct connection of our fulfillment center to our logistics hub in Berlin enables the shipments created by us to be delivered to your customers quickly.

How can we help you?

In order to be able to precisely identify your needs and to be able to offer you a quick and tailor-made solution, we ask you to provide the following information when contacting us:

  • Ø Shipments per month
  • Ø Weight of a shipment
  • Ø Size of the individual good
  • Ø Individual goods in a shipment
  • Estimated number and quantity of individual goods to be stored
