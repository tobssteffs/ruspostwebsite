Customs and VAT

We are available for questions regarding customs and value added tax. Below you will find an overview of customs issues and information related to Russia.

Customs related information in Russia

For most people, customs means a process that triggers a feeling of uncertainty and seems quite complicated. Customs rules between EU and Russia are indeed complex as a whole. However, when dealing with only one product group, this complexity declines significantly.
‍
Nevertheless, please always inform yourself about the current customs regulations. All information is given without any guarantee.

Russia and Eurasian Economic Union (EAEU)

There are restrictions for goods imported to Russia and the Eurasian Economic Union.

The following two categories shall be taken into account:
  • Goods regulated by the EAEU: an exhaustive list is published by the EAEU
  • Export restrictions adopted by the EU and the US:  the list by the Federal Office of Economics and Export Control

200 euro exemption limit

When ordering goods abroad worth more than 200 euros or weighing more than 31 kg, for the excess part one will need to pay a customs duty of 15% of their value, but not less than 2 euros per 1 kg. If the marketplace or onlinestore is integrated with “The Authorized Operator” service on Russian Post website or in the mobile application, the customer can pay online on the store's website. This allows one to quickly and conveniently pay customs duties online without commission, additional fees and a visit to the customs authorities.

All information provided hereinis subject to change
